About

Jenison

Vocals / Guitar

Vocalist and guitarist. Jenison's powerful voice and emotional delivery are the heart of Abunto. All the way from Santa Cruz California, he moved to Fort Collins Colorado to study graphic design and music at CSU blending diverse artistic backgrounds.

Aidan Flanagan

Keys / Piano

Virtuoso pianist known for blistering solos and innovative licks that push the boundaries of funk disco. Raised in Alaska, he moved to Fort Collins Colorado to study neuroscience and music at CSU incorporating his strong intellect with his expressive playing.

Isaac Hermanson

Bass

The groove master who lays down the foundation with melodic bass lines and beautiful harmonies. Home grown in Alaska, he moved to Fort Collins Colorado to study music at CSU spending all of his time perfecting his craft and becoming a master of his instrument.

Aedyn Simon

Drums

He hits circles with sticks bringing energy and creativity from behind the drumset. Only a short drive from Denver Colorado, Aedyn moved to Fort Collins Colorado where he studies computer science and music where he applies his technical skills to help Abunto spread their content.

Our Story

Formed in September of 2024, ABUNTO quickly rose from small club gigs and house shows to major stages with their unique blend of funk, disco, rock, and jazz.

"We started as four friends in the CSU jazz program, never imagining we'd be making such vibrant music. Our music comes from our own experiences as well everyone that has come before us. We take inspiration from everywhere and hope to spread our music around the world."

- Jenison, ABUNTO
